Strategic Evolution

In Session 1, we broke the ice. Teachers focused on reducing the 'blank page' anxiety by using Gemini to draft lessons and resources. The success here was foundational, establishing a common language for prompting across different local authorities.

In Session 2, we evolved into 'Deep Work'. NotebookLM shifted the focus from creation to synthesis. The data shows a significant lean towards academic accuracy, with teachers feeling more confident grounding AI in their own trusted sources (SQA/Policy) rather than broad web data.
